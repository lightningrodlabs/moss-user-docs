# Moss Settings

Moss has **two distinct Settings screens**, and it helps to know which one is which before you go hunting for an option.

This page is about the **app-level** Moss Settings — opened from the **gear icon in the left sidebar**. It controls preferences for Moss as a whole, on your device. Each group has its own separate Settings (the **gear in the group's top bar**) — those are covered in the *Groups* section, since they only affect that group.

## The tabs at a glance

The Moss Settings dialog has five tabs across the top: **Profile**, **Language**, **Notifications**, **Feedback**, and **Danger Zone**. Most of them get their own page in this guide; the short version below points you to the right one.

## Profile

Your **global default profile** — a display name and avatar that Moss uses as a starting point when you join a new group. It lives on your device and isn't shared with any group directly.

See **[Your Profile](./profiles)** for how this default relates to the per-group profile each group sees.

## Language

Pick the language Moss uses for its own UI. Switching here only changes Moss's chrome — menus, dialogs, settings, group home — not the content of any tool. Each tool ships its own translations (or doesn't), independent of this setting.

![The Language tab](/screenshots/settings-language.png)

The list reflects what's been translated so far; more languages are added over time.

## Notifications

Sound settings for incoming notifications — a master toggle, a volume slider, per-urgency level sounds, and custom-sound uploads.

See **[Notification Sounds](./notifications)** for the full breakdown of the urgency levels and which one surfaces where.

## Feedback

A way to send design feedback (with screenshots, if you like) from inside Moss while it's under active development. End users can leave this tab alone; it's mostly here for testers and reviewers.

## Danger Zone

Backup and restore your Moss data, override network configuration, and — if you really mean it — factory-reset everything. The actions on this tab are the ones with the fewest undos, so it has its own dedicated page.

See **[Danger Zone](./danger-zone)** for what each action does, what gets destroyed, and what's recoverable.

## Moss Settings vs. Group Settings

A common source of confusion: Moss has *two* gear icons, and they go to different places.

- **Moss Settings** — the gear in the **left sidebar**. Preferences for the app itself: your default profile, language, notification sounds, your data. Applies across every group.
- **Group Settings** — the gear in **the group's top bar** (next to *Invite People*). Preferences for *one specific group*: its name and icon, the tools installed in it, its members, your per-group profile, and the group's own Danger Zone.

When a guide page says "open Settings," check the context. Most of the time you can tell from where the action lives (your global preferences vs. one group's setup); when in doubt, the breadcrumb above each section in this guide should help.
